Location

Andheri, Mumbai's calm, posh Westren neighbourhood, is one of the city's most prized commercial destinations for the city's elite. This neighbourhood is like a gateway to two worlds: the quaint charm of West Mumbai and the bustling vibe of the suburbs. It is conveniently located just minutes away from high street retail destinations, fine dining restaurants, and business districts. Andheri is the ideal place to work because it is home to some of the city's most prestigious educational and cultural organisations.
